## Key Concepts Learned

- **Phishing:** Fraudulent attempts to obtain sensitive information by impersonating trustworthy entities.
- **Email Spoofing:** Forging email sender addresses to appear as if the email is from a legitimate source.
- **Header Analysis:** Examining email metadata to detect spoofing and other anomalies.
- **Social Engineering:** Manipulating people into performing actions or revealing confidential information.
- **Threat Detection:** Identifying and responding to potential cyber threats.

## Final Thoughts

This task helped me understand how phishing attacks work and how to spot them. I learned the importance of checking sender addresses, links, and email headers, and how to use free tools to analyze suspicious emails. I also improved my skills in reporting and documenting my findings.
